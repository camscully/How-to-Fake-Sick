Directions on How to Fake Sick

  1. Begin to show light symptoms the night before. Act clumsy and start acting sick after dinner.
  2. Do not do something you want to do. An example would be to not eat dessert or finish your favorite meal.
  3. Keep snacks in your room so you don't get too hungry.
  4. Say your stomach hurts when parents ask if you are alright.
  5. Start, but do not finish your homework. If you're doing homework infront of parents, put your head down constantly.
  6. Go to bed early, but do not tell your parents.
  7. Wake up in middle of night and get your parents up to say you just threw up. Even force tears.
  8. All through the night cough loudly and rub your eyes to look sick.
  9. Stay up through the night to get bags under your eyes and it gives an actual reason to stay home.
